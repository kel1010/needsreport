About Us


The Needs Report helps identify the greatest need in communities around the world, so aid and social services organizations can move the right resources to the right places, based on what the people living in those communities feel they need the most.

The Needs Report is a fiscally sponsored project of Empowerment WORKS, a 501c3 nonprofit organization. Donations are tax deductible.
