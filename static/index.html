About Us


The Needs Report helps identify the greatest need in communities around the world, so aid and social services organizations can move the right resources to the right places, based on what the people living in those communities feel the need the most.

CREATE A NEED

Add your need online right now, or text it to 011-987-654-3210 anywhere in the world to get started - we will automatically add it to the Needs Map, so social service organizations know what the people in your community really care about.

NEED NEWS

We're always mining our Needs Data to see what the greatest needs are, all over the world, looking for patterns and distilling all our information into helpful reports for aid workers and social services organizations.
